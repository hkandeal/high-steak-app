# High Steak

**Cook. Rate. Share.**

High Steak is a social app for steak lovers and home cooks who want to show what they made, rate their result, and connect with others who care about great steak.

## The Idea

High Steak turns every steak night into a story:

- Post a photo of your dish
- Add your rating from 1 to 5 stars
- Write a short comment about how it turned out
- Explore other people's meals and inspiration

## What Makes It Fun

- A focused community around one passion: steak
- Quick, visual posts that are easy to browse
- Personal progress over time as your cooking improves
- A simple and friendly place to discover new ideas

## Vision

High Steak is built to be the go-to space for steak culture: from beginners learning their first sear to experienced grill masters sharing signature cuts.
